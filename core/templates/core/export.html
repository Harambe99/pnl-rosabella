Export P&L as CSV

Pick a month and a level of detail. Downloads a CSV you can open in Excel, Sheets, or Numbers.

Monthly-level P&L

One column with the month's totals, plus a % of Net Revenue column for each row. Use this for month-on-month comparisons or to paste into a deck.

Daily-level P&L

One column per day of the chosen month, plus a month-total column. Includes $ and % NR for every cell. Use this for granular review or to dig into specific-day performance.

Notes: empty cells in the CSV correspond to section headers and sub-rows that don't carry a value (e.g., "REVENUE", "Fulfillment"). The dollar values are unrounded — Excel will display them however you format the column. % NR is rounded to 1 decimal.
