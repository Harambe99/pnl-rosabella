Export P&L as Excel

Pick a month and a level of detail. The download is an .xlsx with two sheets — the P&L numbers, plus a Line Item Guide sheet that explains every row. Click any line-item label in the P&L sheet to jump to its explanation.

Monthly-level P&L

One column with the month's totals plus a % of Net Revenue column for each row. Use this for month-on-month comparisons or to paste into a deck.

Daily-level P&L

One column per day of the chosen month plus a month-total column. Includes $ and % NR for every cell. Use this for granular review or specific-day performance.

Notes: blank rows are section dividers ("REVENUE", "Fulfillment", etc.) that don't carry a value. Dollar values display with TikTok's accounting sign convention (negatives in parentheses). Both Daily and Monthly exports include the Line Item Guide sheet — your boss can click any row label to see what that line item means without needing access to the app.
